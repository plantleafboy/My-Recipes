Spicy and sweet noodles

Description

a spicy and sweet sauce mixed with lightly cooked noodles and bok choy

ingredients

  • noodles
  • chilli oil
  • garlic
  • spring onion
  • sauce
    • sugar
    • corn starch
    • light soy + shaoxing wine

steps

  1. boil noodles in water until firm and prepare sauces
  2. cold wash noodles
  3. cook bok choy
  4. mix noodles together with the sauce well
  5. garnish with sesame oil, seeds, spring onion and eat
